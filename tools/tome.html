Tome

Tome is an AI-powered storytelling tool that helps you create presentations, stories, and other visual narratives. It can be used to generate presentations from text, design slides with AI, and add interactive elements to your stories.

How to Use Tome for Students

Tome can be a valuable tool for students who need to create presentations and other visual narratives for their classes. Here are some ways you can use Tome to enhance your storytelling:

  • Presentation Generation: Use Tome to generate a presentation from a text prompt.
  • AI-Powered Design: Use Tome's AI-powered design tools to create beautiful and engaging slides.
  • Interactive Elements: Add interactive elements to your stories, such as quizzes, polls, and videos.

Usage Examples

Here are some examples of how you can use Tome to improve your storytelling:

  • Prompt: "Create a presentation about the life of Martin Luther King Jr."
  • Prompt: "Design a slide about the different types of renewable energy."
  • Prompt: "Add a quiz to my presentation about the planets in our solar system."
