Semantic Scholar

Semantic Scholar is an AI-powered research tool that helps you find and understand scientific literature. It can be used to find relevant papers, get summaries of articles, and identify key findings from the research.

How to Use Semantic Scholar for Students

Semantic Scholar can be a valuable tool for students who are conducting research for their academic work. Here are some ways you can use Semantic Scholar to enhance your research:

  • Literature Search: Use Semantic Scholar to find relevant papers for your research topic.
  • Summarization: Use Semantic Scholar to get summaries of articles and identify the key findings.
  • Cited Answers: Semantic Scholar provides citations for its answers, so you can easily find the source of the information.

Usage Examples

Here are some examples of how you can use Semantic Scholar to improve your research:

  • Prompt: "What are the effects of exercise on mental health?"
  • Prompt: "What is the consensus on the effectiveness of mindfulness for reducing stress?"
  • Prompt: "Find papers that support the theory of general relativity."
