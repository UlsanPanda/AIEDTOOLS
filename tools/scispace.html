SciSpace

SciSpace is an AI-powered research tool that helps you find, understand, and manage academic papers. It can be used to discover relevant literature, get summaries of articles, and organize your research.

How to Use SciSpace for Students

SciSpace is a powerful tool for students who are conducting research for their academic work. Here are some ways you can use SciSpace to enhance your research:

  • Literature Discovery: Use SciSpace to find relevant papers for your research topic. You can search by keyword, author, or even by asking a question in natural language.
  • AI Research Assistant: Use SciSpace's AI assistant, Copilot, to get summaries of articles, ask questions about the content, and get simplified explanations of complex text.
  • Research Management: Use SciSpace to organize your research by saving notes, highlighting important information, and creating a personalized library of papers.
  • Citation Generation: Use SciSpace to generate citations in various formats, including APA, MLA, and Chicago.

Usage Examples

Here are some examples of how you can use SciSpace to improve your research:

  • Search: "What are the latest advancements in renewable energy technology?"
  • Copilot: "Summarize the key findings of this paper on the effects of climate change on biodiversity."
  • Copilot: "Explain the methodology used in this study on the effectiveness of a new cancer treatment."
