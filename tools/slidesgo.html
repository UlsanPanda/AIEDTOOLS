Slidesgo

Slidesgo is an AI-powered presentation maker that helps you create beautiful and engaging presentations. It can be used to generate presentations from text, design slides with AI, and access a library of millions of photos, icons, and other resources.

How to Use Slidesgo for Students

Slidesgo can be a valuable tool for students who need to create presentations for their classes. Here are some ways you can use Slidesgo to enhance your presentations:

  • Presentation Generation: Use Slidesgo's AI presentation maker to generate a presentation from a text prompt.
  • AI-Powered Design: Use Slidesgo's AI-powered design tools to create beautiful and professional-looking slides.
  • Resource Library: Access Slidesgo's library of millions of photos, icons, and other resources to enhance your presentations.

Usage Examples

Here are some examples of how you can use Slidesgo to improve your presentations:

  • Prompt: "Create a presentation about the solar system."
  • Prompt: "Design a slide about the water cycle."
  • Prompt: "Find a photo of a brain for my presentation on psychology."
