Khanmigo

Khanmigo is an AI-powered personal tutor and teaching assistant from Khan Academy. It can help you with a variety of subjects, including math, science, and humanities, by providing personalized guidance and feedback.

How to Use Khanmigo for Students

Khanmigo is a powerful tool for students who want to get one-on-one help with their studies. Here are some ways you can use Khanmigo to enhance your learning:

  • Personalized Tutoring: Get one-on-one help with a variety of subjects. Khanmigo can provide guidance on exercises, videos, and articles within the Khan Academy platform.
  • Writing Coach: Brainstorm ideas, get real-time feedback, and collaborate on writing assignments.
  • Coding Practice: Receive real-time feedback on your code in languages like JavaScript, HTML, Python, and SQL.
  • Test Prep: Prepare for tests by working through problems and thinking critically. Khanmigo avoids giving direct answers, so you can learn to solve problems on your own.

Usage Examples

Here are some examples of how you can use Khanmigo to improve your learning:

  • Math: "I'm stuck on this algebra problem. Can you help me understand how to solve it?"
  • Writing: "Can you give me some feedback on my essay? I'm not sure if my thesis is strong enough."
  • Coding: "I'm getting an error in my Python code. Can you help me debug it?"
