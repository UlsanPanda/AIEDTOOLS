Descript

Descript is an AI-powered audio and video editing platform that makes editing as simple as editing a text document. It can be used to edit podcasts, videos, and other types of audio and video content.

How to Use Descript for Students

Descript can be a valuable tool for students who need to edit audio and video for their schoolwork. Here are some ways you can use Descript to enhance your editing:

  • Podcast Editing: Use Descript to edit your podcasts and remove filler words, mistakes, and other unwanted audio.
  • Video Editing: Use Descript to edit your videos and add titles, transitions, and other effects.
  • Transcription: Use Descript to transcribe your audio and video recordings, so you can easily edit them as text.

Usage Examples

Here are some examples of how you can use Descript to improve your editing:

  • Podcast: "Edit this podcast episode and remove all the 'ums' and 'ahs'."
  • Video: "Add a title and end screen to this video."
  • Transcription: "Transcribe this audio recording of a lecture."
