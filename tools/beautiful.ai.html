Beautiful.ai

Beautiful.ai is an AI-powered presentation maker that helps you create stunning presentations in minutes. It can be used to generate presentations from text, design slides with AI, and collaborate with others on presentations.

How to Use Beautiful.ai for Students

Beautiful.ai can be a valuable tool for students who need to create presentations for their classes. Here are some ways you can use Beautiful.ai to enhance your presentations:

  • Presentation Generation: Use Beautiful.ai to generate a presentation from a text prompt.
  • AI-Powered Design: Use Beautiful.ai's AI-powered design tools to create beautiful and professional-looking slides.
  • Collaboration: Use Beautiful.ai to collaborate with classmates on presentations in real time.
