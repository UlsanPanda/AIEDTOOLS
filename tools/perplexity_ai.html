Perplexity AI

Perplexity AI is an AI-powered search engine that provides direct, sourced answers to questions. It can be used to find information on a variety of topics, get summaries of articles, and brainstorm ideas for research papers.

How to Use Perplexity AI for Students

Perplexity AI can be a valuable tool for students who want to improve their research skills. Here are some ways you can use Perplexity AI to enhance your research:

  • Research: Use Perplexity AI to find information on a variety of topics, get summaries of articles, and brainstorm ideas for research papers.
  • Fact-Checking: Use Perplexity AI to fact-check information and ensure that your sources are reliable.
  • Cited Answers: Perplexity AI provides citations for its answers, so you can easily find the source of the information.
