Scite

Scite is an AI-powered research tool that helps you discover and evaluate scientific articles. It can be used to see how a paper has been cited, get summaries of articles, and find related papers.

How to Use Scite for Students

Scite can be a valuable tool for students who are conducting research for their academic work. Here are some ways you can use Scite to enhance your research:

  • Citation Analysis: Use Scite to see how a paper has been cited by other researchers. This can help you to understand the impact of the paper and to identify related research.
  • Summarization: Use Scite to get summaries of articles and identify the key findings.
  • Literature Discovery: Use Scite to find related papers and to explore the research landscape.

Usage Examples

Here are some examples of how you can use Scite to improve your research:

  • Prompt: "How has this paper on the effects of climate change been cited?"
  • Prompt: "Summarize the key findings of this paper on the effectiveness of a new cancer treatment."
  • Prompt: "Find papers that are related to this paper on the causes of the American Civil War."
