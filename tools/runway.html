Runway

Runway is an AI-powered video editing platform that offers a suite of tools for creating and editing videos. It can be used to generate videos from text, edit videos with AI, and collaborate with others on video projects.

How to Use Runway for Students

Runway can be a valuable tool for students who need to create and edit videos for their schoolwork. Here are some ways you can use Runway to enhance your videos:

  • Video Generation: Use Runway's Gen-2 to generate videos from text prompts.
  • AI Editing: Use Runway's AI-powered editing tools to remove backgrounds, add effects, and edit your videos with ease.
  • Collaboration: Use Runway to collaborate with classmates on video projects in real time.

Usage Examples

Here are some examples of how you can use Runway to improve your videos:

  • Prompt: "Generate a video of a rocket launching into space."
  • Prompt: "Remove the background from this video of me talking."
  • Prompt: "Add a slow-motion effect to this video of a bird flying."
