Otter.ai

Otter.ai is an AI-powered transcription service that can transcribe lectures, meetings, and other audio recordings in real time. It can be used to take notes, review key information, and collaborate with classmates.

How to Use Otter.ai for Students

Otter.ai is a valuable tool for students who want to improve their note-taking and study habits. Here are some ways you can use Otter.ai to enhance your learning:

  • Live Transcription: Use Otter.ai to get a live transcript of your lectures, so you can focus on listening and understanding the material.
  • Automated Summaries: Otter.ai can automatically generate a summary of your recordings, so you can quickly review the key points.
  • Keyword Search: Use Otter.ai's keyword search to find specific information within your recordings.
  • Collaboration: Use Otter.ai to collaborate with classmates on notes by highlighting, commenting, and inserting images within the transcript.

Usage Examples

Here are some examples of how you can use Otter.ai to improve your learning:

  • Record a lecture: Use Otter.ai to record a lecture and get a live transcript.
  • Review a meeting: Use Otter.ai to review a recording of a study group meeting and find key decisions and action items.
  • Collaborate on notes: Use Otter.ai to share a transcript with a classmate and collaborate on notes.
