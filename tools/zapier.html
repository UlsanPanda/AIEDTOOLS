Zapier

Zapier is an automation platform that uses AI to connect thousands of different apps and create automated workflows, known as "Zaps." It can be used to automate repetitive tasks and streamline your workflows, boosting your productivity.

How to Use Zapier for Students

Zapier can be a valuable tool for students who want to automate their workflows and save time on repetitive tasks. Here are some ways you can use Zapier to enhance your productivity:

  • Automate Your To-Do List: Create a Zap that automatically adds new assignments from your learning management system to your to-do list app.
  • Organize Your Notes: Create a Zap that automatically saves your notes from a note-taking app to a cloud storage service.
  • Stay on Top of Your Schedule: Create a Zap that automatically adds new events from your calendar to your to-do list app.

Usage Examples

Here are some examples of how you can use Zapier to improve your productivity:

  • Zap: When a new assignment is posted in Canvas, add it to my Todoist.
  • Zap: When I create a new note in Evernote, save it to my Google Drive.
  • Zap: When a new event is added to my Google Calendar, create a new task in Asana.
