Microsoft 365 Copilot

Microsoft 365 Copilot is an AI-powered assistant that is integrated into Microsoft Office applications. It can be used to draft documents, summarize emails, and analyze data within familiar programs like Word, Excel, and Outlook.

How to Use Microsoft 365 Copilot for Students

Microsoft 365 Copilot can be a valuable tool for students who use Microsoft Office for their schoolwork. Here are some ways you can use Microsoft 365 Copilot to enhance your productivity:

  • Writing Assistance: Use Copilot in Word to draft documents, get feedback on your writing, and improve your grammar and style.
  • Email Management: Use Copilot in Outlook to summarize long emails, draft replies, and manage your inbox.
  • Data Analysis: Use Copilot in Excel to analyze data, create charts and graphs, and identify trends.

Usage Examples

Here are some examples of how you can use Microsoft 365 Copilot to improve your productivity:

  • Word: "Write a five-paragraph essay about the importance of recycling."
  • Outlook: "Summarize the key points from this email thread."
  • Excel: "Create a bar chart showing the sales data for the last quarter."
