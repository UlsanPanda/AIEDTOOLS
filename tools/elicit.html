Elicit

Elicit is an AI-powered research assistant that helps you with literature reviews. It can be used to find relevant papers, extract key information, and identify themes in the research.

How to Use Elicit for Students

Elicit can be a valuable tool for students who are conducting literature reviews for their research papers. Here are some ways you can use Elicit to enhance your research:

  • Literature Search: Use Elicit to find relevant papers for your research topic.
  • Information Extraction: Use Elicit to extract key information from papers, such as the research question, methodology, and findings.
  • Theme Identification: Use Elicit to identify common themes and trends in the research.

Usage Examples

Here are some examples of how you can use Elicit to improve your research:

  • Prompt: "Find papers about the effects of social media on mental health."
  • Prompt: "What are the main themes in the research on climate change and agriculture?"
  • Prompt: "Extract the methodology from this paper on the effectiveness of a new drug."
